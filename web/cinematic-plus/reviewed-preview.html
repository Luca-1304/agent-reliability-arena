Technical underneath

Every visual resolves into something inspectable.

Open each layer to move from the failure to the architecture, representative trace, measured result and honest limitation.

A successful tool receipt may prove that one action occurred without proving the requested outcome. Required fields can be absent, partial evidence can be promoted to completion, and a later rollback can make an earlier success stale.

Completion is not a sentence generated by the system. It is a judgement against observable state.
CLAIM“Done.”

Fluent output from the actor under test.

MISSINGNo destination proof

The required recipient or external state is unobserved.

RISKFalse completion

A downstream process trusts a state that does not exist.

Completion must be judged against a fixed acceptance contract and independently observable state, not the system's own confidence or wording.
